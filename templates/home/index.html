📖 About NoteHub

Empowering Students to Excel Together Through Knowledge Sharing

NoteHub is an all-in-one digital learning and study material sharing platform created for college students. We eliminate the frustration of hunting for quality notes before exams by providing a central, peer-to-peer library of verified study guides, handwritten notes, and lecture materials.

✓
Built-in Digital E-Note & Whiteboard Canvas

Type formatted notes, sketch handwritten diagrams with paint brush & eraser tools, or attach document files in one unified workspace.

✓
Multi-Branch & Semester Categorization

Filter notes by Computer Science, IT, ECE, Mechanical, Civil, BCA/MCA, Commerce, and Semesters 1 through 8.

✓
Community Discussion & Instant Bookmarks

Engage in student discussions, ask questions on notes, save bookmarks for revision, and upvote quality notes.
